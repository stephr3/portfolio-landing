About Me

I've been studying at Epicodus for just a week now, and I'm very excited about taking my coding journey to the next level. I've been coding on and off for about 2 years now. It started when a friend encouraged me to join a group in Portland called PyLadies. I learned some Python and command line with them and had a lot of fun. I started to think I might want to take this coding thing further, so I joined Code Oregon to study via the Treehouse website.

After coding for a while, I went abroad to Vietnam to teach English. After returning, I decided to take the plunge at Epicodus. I hope that I can combine my experience in education and non-profits with my new coding skills to consult with non-profits or work on educational apps.
